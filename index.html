About us

Afghan Rahimi’s offers the highest calibre Afghani Cuisine available in Melbourne. With over 3 decades of culinary experience between the two head chefs, we trully specialise in tantalising Afghani Flavours, everything comes freshly prepared and all dietary requirements are easily catered for. Afghani Flavours are rich but modest and are the perfect example of cross cultural cuisines, with a variety of dishes ranging from mouth watering dumplings (both Vegetarian and Non Vegetarian) and Charcoal cooked Kabab Skewers through to flavoured Rice and Curries. Lunch or Dinnner at Afghan Rahimi’s becomes more than a meal, it becomes an unforgettable experience. BYO. Dear valued customers,We are pleased to offer complimentary tea and desserts with all banquets every Tuesday and Wednesday. Please note that for all groups of 10+ people on weekends, we serve our range of banquets only (with catering for dietary requirements). Ala carte options are available, but require ordering prior to arrival or at time of booking. Both options remain open for smaller groups. Parking is available in the adjoining Multi Level car park at no cost from 6:00pm – 12:00am, Monday-Saturday. Bookings are essential. Opening Hours Please note our trading hours are 5:00pm-10:00pm Tuesday to Sunday. We apologise for the conflicting timing shown elsewhere.
